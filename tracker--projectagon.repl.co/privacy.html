Zombs Tracker Privacy Policy

Zombs Tracker developers collect minimal information from site users to ensure that your privacy is always our top priority. By using the Service to track stats and tags, you are storing your tag in our Database for caching and other important reasons to help make the site function better. Your tag will never be shared outside of the Database, to keep your statistics private.

IP addresses are not collected from site users unless the said user has offended the Terms of Service at which point we may use it to block their access to our site.
